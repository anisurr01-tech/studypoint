I. State whether the following sentences are True or False

  1. The hardest part of schooling, according to Shaw, is the early part. – True
  2. School life is always irksome. – False
  3. There was a time when German boots were not divided into rights and lefts. – True
  4. The scholar who knows everything exists. – False
  5. Shaw forgets everybody five minutes after they have been introduced to him. – True

II. Answer these questions in one sentence

1. What is Shaw's interesting opinion on 'routine'?
Shaw opines that while a routine is designed to suit everyone, in reality, it suits nobody because every student is an individual case needing individual attention.

2. Why were English people going to live in Germany astonished?
They were astonished to discover that German boots were not divided into rights and lefts.
